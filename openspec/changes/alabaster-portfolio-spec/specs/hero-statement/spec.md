## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Professional Identity Alignment
The hero section SHALL explicitly identify the developer as a "Web Systems Developer" rather than a generic "MERN stack developer" or "full-stack engineer".

#### Scenario: First Impression for CTOs
- **WHEN** the homepage loads
- **THEN** the core title MUST be "Web Systems Developer" with supporting copy highlighting "High-Performance Reactivity" and "System Architecture".

### Requirement: Witty and Unfiltered Tone
The hero copy SHALL use a direct, witty, and unfiltered tone that signals seniority and confidence.

#### Scenario: Reading the Hero Statement
- **WHEN** a user reads the hero copy
- **THEN** it MUST emphasize engineering "hard paths" (Rust, Wasm, custom CI/CD) and explicitly exclude corporate fluff.

### Requirement: Respect for the Craft
The portfolio's primary value proposition SHALL center around "Respect for the Craft" and technical depth.

#### Scenario: Value Proposition Check
- **WHEN** a visitor scans the hero section
- **THEN** they MUST see a statement that clearly articulates the developer's commitment to high-quality system design over generic application building.
